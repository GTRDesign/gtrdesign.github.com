DBX Inspiration
Aston Martin Portfolio

The Aston Martin current design language is beautiful, but showing some age. So what is the next step? This project is not to find the lines of Aston Martin's yesterday, but carry on the feeling to the future.

The Aston Martin's key design langauge features on Quality of Materials. Certain materials have certain aesthetics. Metal: large surface planes divided by sharp creases with a reflective lustre. Leather is soft, thin but durable. This brings the high craftsmanship into focus, something that makes Aston Martin stand out.
